Storytelling has always been an important part of my life, and I’ve wanted to be a writer for as long as I can remember.

When I’m not working on my next novel, you’ll probably find me reading, making YouTube videos, and spending time with friends and family.

The Manuscript Atelier

Ghostwriting • Editing

Welcome to the Manuscript Atelier! I’m so excited to share the ghostwriting and editing services I offer for writers and creatives who have a story idea they want to bring to life.

You’ve spent months (maybe years) working on your manuscript, and now you’re ready to take the next step. Editing can often feel like a daunting process, and I firmly believe that getting a second opinion can be a game-changer for your novel. Or maybe you have an idea for a book that you’ve always wanted to write, but you’re not sure where to start.

That’s what I’m here for.

My goal is to help clients elevate their stories and turn their ideas into well-written content. Whether you’re deep in revisions and need a fresh pair of eyes or you’re searching for a writer who can help you write your novel, I would love to collaborate with you on the next stage of your journey.

At The Manuscript Atelier, I work closely with clients to refine their stories and provide clear next steps for moving their projects forward. My aim isn’t just to improve the manuscript, but to help shape something you can be truly proud of.

If you’re ready to take your project to the next level, I’d love to work with you.

Services

Ghostwriting

Do you have a story idea or a message that you want to share, but you don’t have the time or the experience to write it yourself? Ghostwriting allows you to bring your vision to life while I handle the writing process behind the scenes.

The ghostwriting process typically includes:

  • An initial consultation
  • Project planning and outlining
  • Writing the manuscript or content
  • Regular feedback check-ins
  • Revisions

Every ghostwriting project is unique, so pricing depends on the scope and complexity.
